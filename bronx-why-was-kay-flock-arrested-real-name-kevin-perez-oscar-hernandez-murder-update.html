Bronx: Why Was Kay Flock Arrested? Real Name Kevin Perez, Oscar Hernandez Murder Update

There are rumors that Kay Flock has been arrested in the Bronx. It is not the first time that he has been taken into custody. 

On November 13, 2021, he was arrested for possessing guns and weapons. According to police, he used those as a prop for his music video, but they caught him.

Once again, rumors are circulating related to his arrest. Netizens are speculating various things and expressing their sadness over the news.

Why Was Kay Flock Arrested in the Bronx?

It is not confirmed that Kay Flock was arrested in the Bronx. The news came by after NY Daily released arrest news of a boy named Kevin Perez.

Reports say that Kevin Perez, 18, shot and killed 24 years old Oscar Henandez outside a Harlem barbershop. The victim was waiting inside the shop to get the haircut. Perez then walked into the shop and started a verbal altercation with him.

Oscar went outside to confront him, and when he was about to walk away, Kevin pulled the trigger and shot him in the neck and back. Police believe that it was a gang-related shooting. The victim was a member of the Bronx-based Thridside gang.

Moreover, a photo of a police arrest warrants circulating on Twitter. It shows the picture of the rapper, and the statement reads he was in the run after murdering Oscar.

Thus, it seems the rapper has been arrested for the murder charges. Nevertheless, many people cannot believe he has committed such a crime and is in jail.

Kay Flock Real Name Is Kevin Perez

The real name of Kay Flock is Kevin Perez. He also goes by the name of Kay Roy.

Many sources say that he is 28 years old. However, there are also rumors that his real age is 18, but because of the tattoo, he seems to be in his late 20s. People on Reddit have discussed his age. They said that he was born in 2003 and is still a teenager.

Now, many people think he has risked his bright future and career. He was a prominent rapper who was gaining popularity despite his young age.

Oscar Hernandez Murder Update

Oscar Hernandez has been murdered outside a Harlem barbershop. He was shot in the neck and the back.

Kevin Perez has been arrested in relation to the incident. He is awaiting arraignment Thursday in Manhattan Criminal Court.
