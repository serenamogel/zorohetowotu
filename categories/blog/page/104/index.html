published on June 24, 2024
Frederic Arnault  is a French Businessman and CEO at TAG Heuer, a luxury watch brand. He is the son of multi billionaire Bernard Arnault and his wife Hélène Mercier. When he was named CEO of TAG Heuer at the age of 25, Frédéric was the second-youngest Arnault to hold that position. From October 2014 to March 2015, Frédéric Arnault was the Lead Paramedic for the Paris Brigade of Sapeurs-Pompiers. From 2018 to 2020, Frédéric Arnault was Tag Heuer’s Strategy and Digital Director.
