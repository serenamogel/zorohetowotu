How Rich Is Peso Pluma?

published on August 15, 2024
Hailing from Zapopan, Mexico, Peso Pluma isn’t your average regional Mexican artist. Born Hassan Emilio Kabande Laija in 1999, this young singer has breathed new life into the genre, weaving lyrical tapestries that blend traditional corridos with Latin urban swagger. Imagine a dusty desert highway soundtracked by trap beats and a touch of reggaeton, that’s Peso Pluma’s signature style. Nicknamed “La Doble P,” Peso Pluma’s rise has been meteoric.
