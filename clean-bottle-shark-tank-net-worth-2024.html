The Founding of Clean Bottle

Clean Bottle was founded with a simple yet innovative idea: to create a water bottle that was easy to clean and prevented the buildup of bacteria and mold. The founder, Dave Mayer, was an avid cyclist who was frustrated with the difficulty of cleaning traditional water bottles. This led to the creation of the Clean Bottle, which features a removable bottom for easy cleaning.

Breakthrough on Shark Tank

The company’s breakthrough came when Dave Mayer appeared on Shark Tank. His pitch, along with a memorable entrance with individuals dressed in bottle costumes, caught the attention of the sharks, particularly Mark Cuban. The investment from Cuban helped propel Clean Bottle into the spotlight and significantly boosted its sales and brand recognition.
