Evin Ahmad was born 8 June 1990 to Kurdish parents i.e., her father was an actor from Sulaymaniyah in Iraq, and her mother, from Afrin in Syria. Evin is an actress and author. She was brought up in Stockholm’s Akalla district, where she spent 22 years of her life. Her first appearance on screen was Ett Öga Rött (2007) which was a film adapted from novel of Jonas Hassen Khemiri.

After her debut, she couldn’t stop herself from making great performances which you can see in the Danish post-apocalyptic series i.e., The Rain and the Swedish crime drama Quicksand. Evin Ahmad gained enormous fame from her role as Leya in Snabba Cash Netflix’s original series. After all this, now you can see her as Erin Carter in the all new Netflix series i.e., Who Is Erin Carter?.
