Fantana - Age, Family, Bio

Afro-beat, Afro-pop, and dancehall singer best known for her songs "Girls Hate On Girls" and "Rich Gyal Anthem." She rose to fame after her role in Netflix's reality show Young, Famous & African season 2. Her song "So What" has garnered over 60,000 streams on Spotify.

Before Fame

She was born with the name Francine Koffie. "So What" was the first song she released, and it came out in the year 2019.

Trivia

She's generated a following of 650,000 through her Instagram account iamfantana. She has a following of 10,000 on her YouTube channel FantanaMusic. 

Family Life

She's from Atlanta, Georgia. She's of Ghanaian descent. Her mother is Dorcas Affo Toffey. Her sister is Lynelle Koffi.

Associated With

She appeared on Young, Famous & African alongside Diamond Platnumz.
