Gemma Ward Career: Model to Actress

The 31 years old model, as well as an actress, has a quite unique start-up in career. Actually, Gemma involvement with modeling was, in fact, an accidental one.

Moving back to the time, in 2002, while accompanying her friends to the Australian modeling competition Search for a Supermodel, Gemma was discovered by a fellow agent at the age of 14. Ward once mentioned in a Teen Vogue interview, that she ended up being scouted herself:

“I had come straight from my auntie and uncle’s farm, and I was wearing this big gray barn jacket with mud all over it. When the scout came up to me, I said, ‘No, thank you.’ They forged my mum’s signature [for mandatory parental consent], and pushed me in front of the cameras.”

So, it was the beginning, and soon she made her Australian Fashion Week debut at the age of fifteen years old. Above all, Ward became the youngest model to grace the cover of Vogue.
