Early Life and Career Beginnings

George Noory was born on June 4, 1950, in Detroit, Michigan. He developed an interest in the mysterious and unexplained at a young age, which later influenced his career choice. He started his career in radio broadcasting while still in the University of Detroit, working as a newscaster on the student-run station.

Professional Career

Noory’s professional career began in earnest in the 1970s. He worked for various radio stations across the country, honing his skills and building a reputation. His big break came in 2003 when he took over from Art Bell as the host of Coast to Coast AM.

Coast to Coast AM

Coast to Coast AM is a late-night radio talk show that covers a variety of topics, most notably those related to the paranormal, conspiracy theories, and other unexplained phenomena. Noory’s engaging style and open-minded approach to these topics have made the show incredibly popular, with millions of listeners tuning in every night.
