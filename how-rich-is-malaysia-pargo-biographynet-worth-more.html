How Rich Is Malaysia Pargo: Biography,Net Worth &amp; more

Malaysia Pargo is the ex-wife of basketball player Jannero Pargo and a well-known Afro-American reality television personality of ‘Basketball Wives: Los Angeles’. In recent times, she has been recognized as one of the most popular television personalities. Aside appearing on TV, she is a great jewelry designer and entrepreneur who has her own lines namely ‘3BeatsL’ and ‘Three Beats Jewelry’.

Who is Malaysia Pargo?

Malaysia Pargo is an American Television personality and a jewelry designer from Compton, California in the United States. She was born on August 12, 1980. She had a younger brother named Dontae Hayes who was shot and killed by the police on New Year’s Eve of December 2013 when he was only 20 years old. Malaysia’s cousin is basketball player Byron Scott. Malaysia was 22 years old when she met her ex-husband, Jannero Pargo, at a party in Los Angeles, California. She has three children and two of them are twins. The couple officially divorced in June 2016 after a 17 month separation.

Malaysia Pargo first appeared on television on the reality television series ‘Basketball Wives: Los Angeles’. Broadcast on VH1, this show is known for featuring a group of women who are associated with professional basketball players. Well, Malaysia became a part of this show because of being associated with her husband, the basketball player Jannero Pargo whom she had been dating since the age of 22. She totally rocked the show and became one of its most popular cast members.

As of July 2017, Malaysia holds about 1.6m followers on Instagram and about 358k followers on Twitter.
