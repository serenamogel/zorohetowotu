John Deere 820 Price, Specs, Review, Horsepower, Weight, Engine Oil Capacity, Attachments, History & Pictures

John Deere 820 Utility Tractor is equipped with a John Deere diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled 151.9 ci [2.5 L] engine, which produces 32 hp [23.9 kW] power at a rated rpm 2100.

John Deere 820 Utility Tractor featured a John Deere 2.5L 3-cyl diesel engine, 7 forward and 1 reverse gear, 4×2 2WD, 31 hp [23.1 kW] PTO Power, and 4000 lbs [1814 kg] weight and 6.5 Qts [6.2 L] oil capacity.

John Deere 820 Utility Tractor attachments include a blade, front-end loader, and backhoe.
