Norah Jones has an estimated net worth of $25 million. The versatile and multi-talented singer, songwriter, actress as well as pianist is the daughter of Indian musician, composer, and sitar master Pandit Ravi Shanker and Sue Jones and is the half-sister of Anoushka Sharma.

Born on 30th March 1979, Norah Jones debuted her career with her solo album Come Away With Me which released in 2002 and won magnanimous 5 Grammy awards and sold over 26 million copies worldwide. Soon she ruled the charts and became an international icon and has since produced hits after hits effortlessly. Her album Feels likes Home sold a million copies while her 2007 album Not Too Late sold about 1.5 million copies. Her 2009 album The Fall also sold over a million copies and gained Platinum status. She also debuted in My Blueberry Nights in 2007 and she has also won Best Actress award in 2009. She was named The Top Jazz artist by Billboard for a span of 2000-2009. Overall her albums have sold more than 50 million albums worldwide till date.
