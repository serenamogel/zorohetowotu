Nse Ikpe Etim Biography: Age, Family, Movies, Husband, &amp; Photos

Nse Ikpe Etim is an award winning Nigerian actress, television personality and entrepreneur born on October 21, 1974.

Etim is the first child in a family of six children.

She grew up in Lagos state but originally hails from Akwa Ibom state south southern part of Nigeria.

Nse Ikpe attended Awa Nursery school and Command Primary School in Kaduna State. She had her secondary education at St Louis college and Federal Government College in Jos, Plateau state before finally completing her education at Federal Government College in Ilorin. Etim obtained a bachelor’s degree in Theater Arts from the University of Calabar in Rivers State.

She’s quite beautiful, talented and skillful, having carved a niche for herself with an excellent interpretation of roles and perfect delivery of scripts.

Nse Ikpe Etim started her career at the age of 18 while acting on stage during her University days. She came to limelight with her role in the blockbuster movie “Reloaded” starring alongside Ramsey Nouah, Desmond Elliot, Rita Dominic and Ini Edo. She has also starred in numerous movies such as Emotional Crack, Venom of Justice, Journey to self, Black November, Broken, The Meeting, Phone Swap, Spell Bound, Mr and Mrs, Kiss and Tell and many others.

She has worked with top actors across the country which include the likes of Nonso Diobi, Yul Edochie, Joseph Benjamin, Majid Michel, Mike Ezeruonye, Ken Erics, John Dumelo, Wale Ojo and so on.

Etim got married to her husband Clifford Sule at a Lagos Registry on February 14, 2013. They had their traditional and white wedding at Akwa Ibom and Lagos state respectively.

Etim has won numerous awards in the course of her career such as Best Actress film at 2010 Nigeria Entertainment Awards, Best Actress in a lead role at 2011 Best of Nollywood Awards, Best Actress in a supporting role at 2012 Nollywood Movies Awards, Best Actress in a Leading role at 2015 Best of Nollywood Awards, Best Actress at 2015 Golden Icons Academy Movie Awards.

Nse Ikpe Etim is one of the most influential Nigerian actress with an estimated net worth of $600,000.
