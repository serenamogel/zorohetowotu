Is T silent in dentist? No, neither of the t’s in “dentist” is silent. Dentist= DENT-IST. Dental= DENT-al. Both T’s are sounded. How do the British say dentist? How do you say dentist in America? If the T is stuck between two vowels, it is pronounced as a D. If it is after an N, it is not pronounced. I heard people pronounce “dentist” as “denist”.
