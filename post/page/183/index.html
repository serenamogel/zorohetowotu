Nicolas Winding Refn Delivers Distinct Vision of Too Old to Die Young | TV/Streaming

published on April 04, 2024
As anyone familiar with “Only God Forgives” and “The Neon Demon” will suspect, “Too Old to Die Young,” which Refn wrote with Ed Brubaker is an exercise in excessive style. Long takes, affectless line delivery, outbursts of extreme violence – the exaggerated delivery of “Too Old to Die Young” is part of the process. Storytelling takes a back seat to style and mood – again, not unlike David Lynch’s TV masterpiece, a show in which the compositions, feelings, and style supplanted traditional narrative. Tory Lanez Mugshot Is Released Transferred To State Prison For Shooting Incident

published on April 04, 2024
Canadian rapper Tory Lanez, legally known as Daystar Peterson, was recently transferred to North Kern State Prison in Delano, California, where he will continue to serve his 10-year sentence. The development follows his conviction for shooting American rapper Megan Thee Stallion in the feet during a heated altercation back in 2020. The New Mugshot and Its Implications Lanez made public his new mugshot just five days after Judge David Herriford denied his bail request; Lanez wanted the court to allow him to live with his wife and child while their legal team filed their appeal; Judge Herriford declined citing Lanez’s conviction of violent felony, violation of court orders history as well as foreign citizenship as reasons behind this decision.
