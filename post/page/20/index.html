Jackson Falls offers many opportunities for technical climbing and is a beautiful scenic area of the Shawnee National Forest. Located near the town of Ozark in the Shawnee National Forest, Jackson Falls offers arguably the best rock climbing in southern Illinois. There are roughly 60 climbing areas along the sandstone bluffs and freestanding boulders, each with multiple named routes on them. There are over 500 named routes in all.
