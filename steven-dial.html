Steven Dial

Steven Dial is an American Journalist. He currently serves as a news anchor and reporter at Fox 4 News. He worked at WLTX, where he won an Edward R. Murrow Award. Steven is a member of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. and the National Association of Black Journalists.

Steven got his first job as a reporter in South Carolina’s WLTX. In 2015, he started working with First Coast News in Jacksonville, Florida. There he covered the 2016 presidential campaign of Donald Trump.
