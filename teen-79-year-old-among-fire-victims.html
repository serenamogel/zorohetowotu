Teen, 79-year-old among fire victims | News, Sports, Jobs

The Maui Police Department confirmed two more victims of the Aug. 8 Lahaina fire on Wednesday as 14-year-old Keyiro Fuentes and 79-year-old Maurice Buen, both of Lahaina.

Fuentes is among the youngest victims to be identified in the aftermath of the fire. Last month, police also named 7-year-old Tony Takafua as one of the victims.

The death toll still stands at 115. So far, 63 individuals have been identified and their families notified. Another five have been identified, but their families have not been located or notified, police said.
