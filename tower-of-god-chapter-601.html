Plotlines & Story

Tower of God is a webtoon series that tells the story of Bam, a young boy who enters a mysterious tower in search of his friend Rachel. The tower is a vast and mysterious place, where each floor is a different world with its own rules, inhabitants, and challenges. Those who climb the tower can obtain anything they desire, such as wealth, power, fame, or even the answer to their existence. However, the tower is also full of dangers, secrets, and enemies, and only a few can reach the top.

Bam is an irregular, a rare and special being who was not chosen by the tower, but entered it by his own will. He has a unique talent and potential for using shinsu, the mystical energy that flows throughout the tower. He also possesses several powerful and mysterious items, such as the thorn fragments, the black march, and the souls of Hoaqin’s victims. He is the target of many factions and individuals, who either want to use him, kill him, or help him.

Throughout his journey, Bam meets various allies and enemies, who have their own motives and backgrounds. He forms a team with Khun, a cunning and loyal strategist from one of the 10 Great Families, and Rak, a proud and strong warrior from the Wraithraiser race. He also encounters other regulars, rankers, slayers, princesses, and guardians, who influence his fate and the fate of the tower. He faces many trials and tests, such as the Crown Game, the Hide and Seek Test, the Workshop Battle, the Hell Train, the Name Hunt Station, the Floor of Death, the Hidden Floor, the Last Station, the Cage, the Wall of Peaceful Coexistence, and the Nest.

Bam’s ultimate goal is to find Rachel, the girl who raised him in a dark cave outside the tower, and who betrayed him and pushed him off the tower. He wants to know why she did that, and what she really wants from the tower. He also wants to discover his own identity and purpose, and to change the tower for the better.
