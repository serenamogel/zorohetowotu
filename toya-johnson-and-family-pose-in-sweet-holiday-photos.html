TOYA JOHNSON AND FAMILY POSE IN SWEET HOLIDAY PHOTOS

Toya Johnson and her family are ready for Christmas. The Friends and Family Hustle star recently shared candids from the family’s photoshoot with her Instagram fans.

“Happy Holidays from my family to yours,” Toya exclaimed in the caption of her social media post. The picture showed Johnson with her fiance, Robert Rushing, and daughters, Reign Rushing and Reginae Carter. Also photographed were Toya’s niece, Jashae, and Robert’s daughter from a previous relationship. Robert has another daughter named Gabriella from a previous relationship who is not featured in the family’s latest photo session.

Toya Johnson and Robert Rushing will soon join their families in matrimony. Robert popped the question to Toya earlier this year in a memorable segment of Friends and Family Hustle. The businessman went all out with a helicopter and proposal that Toya could literally see from the clouds.

“We’re engaged,” Toya exclaimed during the recap segments of Friends and Family Hustle that featured Robert’s proposal. “We’re locked in, baby,” the celebrity mom added. “I want to spend the rest of my life with you,” Toya told Robert.

Toya Johnson and Robert Rushing have one daughter, Reign Rushing. Reginae Carter is Toya’s daughter with rapper Lil Wayne. Stay tuned for more celebrity family holiday news!
