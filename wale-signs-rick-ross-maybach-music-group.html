Wale Signs To Rick Ross Maybach Music Group

Recommended Videos

After months of speculation, Wale confirms through Twitter that he has officially signed onto Rick Ross‘ Maybach Music Group, which is currently under the Def Jam umbrella. Though the label is currently in a bidding war, Wale confirms that Roc Nation will still handle all of the DC rapper’s management duties.

Maybach Music Group is currently home to Triple C’s, Young Breed and Gunplay.
