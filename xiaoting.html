Xiaoting is a Chinese singer, musician, dancer, and model known for being a member of the South Korean girl group Kep1er. Formed through the Mnet reality survival competition show Girls Planet 999 in 2021, the group made its official debut with the extended play named First Impact on January 3, 2022. The group’s discography also consists of other extended plays and tracks such as Wa Da Da, Up!, Doublast, Fly-Up, Troubleshooter, and We Fresh.

Xiaoting Facts

  • She has credited her mother as her role model.
  • Xiaoting’s fellow Kep1er groupmates have been Yujin, Mashiro, Chaehyun, Dayeon, Hikaru, Huening Bahiyyih, Youngeun, and Yeseo.
  • With Kep1er, Xiaoting released the group’s debut extended play titled First Impact on January 3, 2022, through Wake One Entertainment. It includes songs like See the Light, Wa Da Da, MVSK, Another Dream, Shine, and O.O.O (Over&Over&Over). The EP peaked at #1 on the South Korean Albums (Gaon) chart, at #2 on the Japanese Albums (Oricon) chart, at #10 on the Japanese Hot Albums (Billboard Japan) chart, and at #19 on the Finnish Albums (Suomen virallinen lista) chart. Also, it was certified ‘Platinum’ in South Korea.
  • On June 20, 2022, Kep1er released its 2nd extended play Doublast via Wake One Entertainment and it peaked at #2 on the South Korean Albums (Gaon) chart, at #7 on the Finnish Physical Albums (Suomen virallinen lista) chart, at #9 on the Japanese Albums (Oricon) chart, and at #24 on the Japanese Hot Albums (Billboard Japan) chart. Its tracklist consists of Up!, Le Voya9e, Good Night, Rewind, and Attention.
  • The girl group Kep1er released its 3rd extended play Troubleshooter through Wake One Entertainment on October 13, 2022. It was certified ‘Platinum’ in South Korea and peaked at #2 on the South Korean Albums (Circle) chart, at #6 on the Japanese Albums (Oricon) chart, at #7 on the Japanese Combined Albums (Oricon) chart, and at #21 on the Japanese Hot Albums (Billboard Japan) chart.
